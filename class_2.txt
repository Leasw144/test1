Meats

Attributes
1. meat-type (string)
2. temperature (float)
3. meat-ready-to-serve (integer)
4. date-of-purchase (date)

Method
1. calculate-temperature (calculates temperature)
2. method of cooking (roast if pork, braise if beef, etc.)
3. defrost (if meat is under 30 degrees, take meat out of fridge and defrost until temperature is 30 degrees)
4. throw-out (if meat was purchased 2 weeks after date of purchase, throw out)
