Meats

Attributes
1. meat-type (string)
2. temperature (float)
3. meat-ready-to-serve (integer)
4. date-of-purchase (date)

Method
1. calculate-temperature (calculates temperature)
2. method of cooking (roast if pork, braise if beef, etc.)
3. defrost (add meat out for defrost dependent on current meats ready for use)
4. throw-out (if meat temp purchased 2 weeks after date of purchase, throw out)
